When creating a color palette, consider using complementary colors. These are colors that are opposite each other on the color wheel. They create a vibrant and harmonious contrast in your designs.
Use warm colors, such as reds and oranges, to create a sense of excitement and energy in your designs. These colors tend to grab attention and can be useful for highlighting important elements.
Cool colors, like blues and greens, have a calming effect and are great for creating a sense of tranquility and relaxation. using them in spaces where you want to promote a peaceful atmosphere.
Pay attention to the psychological impact of colors. For example, blue is often associated with trust and reliability, while yellow can evoke feelings of happiness and optimism. Use this knowledge to influence the mood of your designs.
